Blueprint 2017
Design, branding, and marketing
blueprint.hackmit.org

Blueprint is a weekend-long learnathon and hackathon for local high school students held at MIT's campus where high schoolers can learn from MIT students, make new friends, and build something cool!

Blueprint was organized by the HackMIT organizing committee, a group of 20-30 MIT undergraduates with the goal of empowering high school students through technology and education. I was part of the Marketing committee, where I helped make branding and design decisions for the event.
