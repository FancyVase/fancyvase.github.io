Stubborn
An Interactive Clock
3D Printing
Laser Cutting
Arduino

Stubborn is an interactive clock that is animated and alive. Its hands roam randomly, alert users when they have an event, and dance to music; only showing the time when the clock is touched.

Design Process
Brainstorming

I built Stubborn for my final project in 4.031: Design Studio - Objects & Interaction, where we were tasked with building a physical representation of time that users could interact with.
