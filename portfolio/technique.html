MIT Technique

Technique is a student group at MIT dedicated to photography, design, and crafting the institute's yearbook.

I worked as Technique's publicity director to manage design, printing, and distribution of membership recruitment and yearbook publicity for the club; working on posters, mailings, and flyers.

Being a part of Technique to craft the yearbook was awesome and seeing everyone be passionate about photography and design was super inspiring and led me to pursuing design and photography further.
